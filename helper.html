About GreaTest Helper App

This App was conceived due to a problem on the management of the GreaTest Card Game gameplay and it's part of TER Project .

The GreaTest Helper can keep track on players' pontuation by name, point counter, a dice and a 20 seconds timer.

The app will soon be available to download for free, jointly with all other resourses associated with GreaTest Card Game.
